Our Journey

Connecting the World Through Smart Trade

GlobexSky was founded in 2018 with a simple but ambitious vision: to eliminate the friction in international trade. We saw small businesses struggling with unreliable suppliers, opaque shipping, and complex payments — and we built the platform to fix it.

Today, our platform connects buyers and suppliers across 150+ countries, handles end-to-end logistics, and processes millions of dollars in secure transactions every day. We're not just a marketplace — we're your global trade partner.

From our AI-powered supplier verification to real-time shipment tracking, every feature we build is designed to give you confidence in every deal you make.
