1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow websites to remember your actions and preferences (such as login credentials, language, and display settings) over a period of time, so you do not have to re-enter them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

Similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage may also be used for the same purposes as cookies. This policy covers all such technologies.

2 Why We Use Cookies

Globex Sky uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • To operate and secure our platform (strictly necessary cookies)
  • To remember your language, currency, and other preferences
  • To analyse how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • To personalise content and advertisements
  • To enable social media features and integrations
  • To provide our real-time chat and customer support features

3 Types of Cookies We Use

By Duration:

  • Session cookies — Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used to maintain your session while browsing.
  • Persistent cookies — Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. Used to remember your preferences across visits.

By Category:

  • Necessary — Essential for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics — Help us understand how visitors interact with the site. Disabled by default.
  • Marketing — Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaigns. Disabled by default.
  • Preferences — Remember your settings such as language, currency, and display options. Disabled by default.

7 Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to erasure — You can request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to object — You can object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing.
  • Right to withdraw consent — You can withdraw your consent to non-essential cookies at any time.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You can lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

For data rights requests, please visit your Account Settings or contact us directly.
