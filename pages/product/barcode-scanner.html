Barcode Scanner

How to scan

  1. Allow camera access when the browser prompts you.
  2. Hold the barcode or QR code inside the green frame.
  3. Keep the code steady and well-lit; use the torch button in dark environments.
  4. The app will auto-detect and search the product instantly.
  5. Can't scan? Use the manual entry field below or upload a photo from your gallery.

Supported formats: EAN-13, UPC-A, Code 128, QR Code, EAN-8, UPC-E, Code 39
