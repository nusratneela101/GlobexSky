Product Overview

Experience audio like never before with the Premium Wireless Earbuds Pro X500. Engineered for audiophiles and everyday listeners alike, the Pro X500 delivers deep bass, crisp highs and a wide stereo soundstage — all without a single wire in sight. Whether you are commuting, working out or taking calls, these earbuds are built to keep up with your active lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) up to -30 dB
  • 30-hour total battery life (8 hrs + 22 hrs case)
  • Bluetooth 5.3 with multipoint pairing
  • IPX5 water & sweat resistance
  • 11 mm custom dynamic driver per ear
  • Touch controls with voice assistant support
  • Transparency / ambient sound mode
  • USB-C fast charge (15 min = 2 hrs playback)
  • Low-latency gaming mode (< 60 ms)
  • Dual-mic ENC for crystal-clear calls
  • Companion app with 10-band EQ
  • Ergonomic in-ear fit, 3 tip sizes included

Design & Build Quality

The Pro X500 features a slim, lightweight profile crafted from matte-finished ABS with a soft-touch coating that resists fingerprints. Each earbud weighs just 5.2 g, and the magnetic charging case slips easily into any pocket at only 48 g. Available in four colour-ways — Ocean Blue, Midnight Black, Pearl White and Coral Red — there is a finish to match every style.
