Wireless Bluetooth Headphones Pro X500

The Wireless Bluetooth Headphones Pro X500 deliver studio-grade audio performance in a premium over-ear design. Engineered for audiophiles and professionals alike, these headphones combine advanced noise cancellation technology with 40-hour battery life, making them ideal for long-haul travel, office use, or immersive home listening sessions.

Equipped with 40mm custom-tuned dynamic drivers, the X500 produces deep, punchy bass alongside crisp highs and a detailed midrange. The adaptive active noise cancellation (ANC) system uses dual microphones to analyze ambient sound and neutralize it in real time, giving you an uninterrupted sonic experience no matter your environment.

The foldable, lightweight architecture — crafted from aircraft-grade aluminum and premium vegan leather — ensures durability and comfort for extended wear. A multi-point connection feature allows simultaneous pairing with two devices, while the built-in voice assistant support works seamlessly with Google Assistant, Siri, and Alexa.
