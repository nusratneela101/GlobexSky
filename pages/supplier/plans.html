Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about supplier plans.

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for Professional?
Yes! Professional comes with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll only be charged once you decide to continue after the trial period.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPal, bank transfers, and Alipay for our international customers. Enterprise customers can also arrange invoice-based billing.
How does the annual billing discount work?
When you choose annual billing, you save 20% compared to monthly pricing. The full annual amount is billed upfront. For Professional, that's $159/mo ($1,908/year) instead of $199/mo ($2,388/year).
What happens to my products if I downgrade?
If you downgrade from Professional to Basic, products beyond the 50-listing limit will be paused (not deleted). You can reactivate them by upgrading again or by deleting other listings to stay within the limit.
Does verification status affect my plan?
The Verified Pro badge is included with both Professional and Enterprise plans. You still need to complete the verification process, but the badge is unlocked once you're on one of these paid plans and your application is approved.
