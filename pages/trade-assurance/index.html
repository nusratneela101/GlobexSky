What Is Trade Assurance?

Item Not Received

Get a full refund if your goods never arrive.

Item Not as Described

Receive compensation if the product differs from listing.

Quality Issues

Covered if goods arrive damaged or defective.

How Trade Assurance Works
1
Place an Eligible Order
Buy from Trade Assurance–verified suppliers. The shield icon marks eligible listings.
2
Receive Your Goods
Your payment is held securely. Once you confirm receipt, the supplier is paid.
3
File a Claim if Needed
If there's a problem, file a claim within the claim window with supporting evidence.
4
Get a Refund
Our team reviews the claim. Approved claims are refunded within 3–5 business days.
Your Payment Is Protected

All Trade Assurance payments are held in a secure escrow-like system. Funds are only released to the supplier after you confirm satisfactory delivery — or refunded to you if a valid claim is approved.
