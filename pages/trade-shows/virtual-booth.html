About Shenzhen TechCo Ltd.

Founded in 2008, Shenzhen TechCo Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of IoT components, smart sensors, and consumer electronics based in Shenzhen, China. With over 1,200 employees and a 42,000 m² production facility, we supply to buyers in 68 countries.

Our products are certified under CE, RoHS, FCC, and ISO 9001 standards. We specialize in custom OEM/ODM manufacturing with lead times as short as 15 days for standard products.
